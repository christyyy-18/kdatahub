Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

+

You can track your order in real-time by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You'll see the current status and estimated delivery date for each order.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied, simply contact our support team with your order number and we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

How long does delivery take?

+

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for 1-2 business days. The delivery timeframe will be shown at checkout based on your location.

Can I cancel or modify an order?

+

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placement. After that, your order will be in processing. For urgent changes, please contact our support team immediately.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

+

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. For orders of 10 or more units, please contact our sales team at sales@kdatahub.com for a custom quote.

Is my payment information secure?

+

Absolutely! We use industry-leading SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance to ensure your payment information is always secure. Your data is never stored on our servers.
